Distributed Control Systems

Distributed Control Systems (DCS) are a category of industrial automation systems used to control complex processes in industries like manufacturing, power plants, oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical production. Unlike centralized systems, a DCS distributes control functions across multiple controllers throughout the plant, improving reliability, scalability, and performance.
